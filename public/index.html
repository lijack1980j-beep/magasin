About Us

Glitch Signal is a multidisciplinary design studio based in Finland. We specialise in crafting compelling user interfaces, intuitive user experiences, and elegant architectural spaces — both interior and exterior.

With over six years of professional experience, our team blends digital product design with spatial thinking. This unique cross‑disciplinary perspective allows us to deliver cohesive solutions that elevate brands and enrich everyday life.
